Florida Women's


Hall of Fame

Since 1992, each year, women from Florida up to three women are nominated and inducted into the Florida's Women Hall of Fame.

“….to recognize and honor those women who, through their works and lives, have made significant contributions to the improvement of life for women and for all citizens of Florida”.
